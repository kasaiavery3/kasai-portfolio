Saika Comic Central

This is a bookmarking app that allows you to find new comics to enjoy. Create an account in order to save them to your wishlist and/or flag ones you have already finished. You are also able to view individual comics and leave comments.

You can find the installation instructions to this repo on Github and the hosted site on Heroku.

LANGUAGES AND DEPENDENCIES:
- Node.js
- Axios
- Bcrypt
- Connect-flash
- Dotenv
- Ejs
- Express-Ejs-Layouts
- Express-Session
- Passport
- Passport-Local
- Sequelize
- Sequelize-Cli
- db = PostgreSQL

FEATURES:
- Wishlist:
Add specific comics you are interested in to your Wishlist via the "Add to Wishlist" button on the corresponding comics' card. You can view and delete items from your Wishlist on the Profile page.
-Finished List:
Add specific comics you have already read to your Finished list via the "Add to Finished" button on the corresponding comics' card. You can view and delete items from your Finished list on the Profile page.
View Comics and Comments:
You can view any individual comic via the "Read" button on the corresponding comics' card. While viewing, you can read and/ or write your own comments that are unique to that specific comic.
